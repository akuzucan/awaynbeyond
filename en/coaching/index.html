Let me be your travel coach!

I have been travelling the world for many years and have made dozens of trips full of joy, excitement and peace.  Over the years, I have spent a lot of time planning and organizing my trips so that I could make each holiday perfect. As a result, I have gained a lot of experience that I would like to share with you so that you can have your own perfect holiday.

Planning a trip is an exciting thing to do but can be very overwhelming if you don’t have enough time to do it or if you simply just don’t know where or how to start. Of course you could go to a travel agent and buy a trip with them but why not have your trip tailored to your own personal specifications?

What can I do for you?

I can organize your trip according to your time, destination, period of travel, budget and interests. With your customized itinerary and armed with my tips specific to the area you are travelling to, you can take off without worries or questions in your head and just enjoy your time.

After hiring me as your travel coach, you will have all the information you need to live your dream holiday.

How does my coaching work?

1

Consultation. First, fill the questionnaire – see blue button below. That way, I will then have some basic information about you and your desired trip. We can then schedule a consultation session via skype, email or phone.

2

Detailed itinerary. Give me 1-2 weeks of time in order to organize your trip and prepare your holiday proposal. Within this proposal, will be an itinerary of your trip, maps, suggestions about hotels and transportation, and a list of places to see each with an accompanying description. I’ll also provide suggestions for restaurants and bars.  Activities which are specifically interesting for you. And most importantly what to pack. I will provide the proposal via email or post if preferred.

3

Support. I will also provide you ongoing support if you want to make minor changes to your itinerary or have further questions.
