About me

“Broad, wholesome, charitable views of men and things cannot be acquired by vegetating in one little corner of the earth all of one’s lifetime.” – Mark Twain

Travelling is magical. It is not just about packing and going away. It’s beyond that. It is about opening your heart to people you don’t know, cultures you don’t understand and places you don’t feel accustomed to. It is about becoming tolerant, loving and learning to appreciate.

Leave home, and you will discover life from a different perspective. Travelling is the best part in human life.

I think the best thing about travelling is its unpredictability. You never know what is waiting for you around the next corner.

My name is Asena Kuzucan. I was born and raised in Istanbul and I lived there until I moved to Austria to study physics at the Vienna University of Technology. Being based in the centre of Europe, I used the opportunity to travel around and to gain a sense of adventure. After graduating from university, I moved to Geneva to complete my PhD and to work.  I still live there.

I have now been travelling for many years. When I was younger I would make short trips for 1 or 2 weeks. I soon began to make month long trips but that still wasn’t enough for me.  I always dreamed of a long-term trip, without that “my holiday is almost over” feeling. Finally in August 2014 I quit my job and took off for 9 months non-stop. I still have a lot to discover and I am eagerly looking forward to my next destination.

I love nature and animals more than anything else. So my trips tend to involve trekking, Natural Park visits, safaris, etc. I love talking to people to learn more about their culture. And even though being vegetarian, I sometimes struggle with food; I love to try new foods and drinks.  I am somewhat of an adventurist and like to travel off the beaten tracks.

I can’t stop thinking about my next trips and this is the fuel of my life. As you know by now how much I love travelling, please have a look around the site and read my blog to share my passion for travelling.

I have visited 49 countries. That is 23.79% of the world!

“Once a year, go someplace you’ve never been before.” – Dalai Lama
